  • The 7 Up 7 Down is a game played with three buttons displayed on the screen.
  • The three options include less than 7, greater than 7, and equal to 7.
    • Each player starts the game by placing their chips on one or two of these options before the two dice are rolled.
  • Numbers below 7 (1,2,3,4,5,6) are known as Seven Down. If you win by calling a Seven Down, the add up is 1x the balance score.
  • Numbers above 7 (8,9,10,11,12) are known as Seven Up. If you win by calling a Seven Up, the add up is 1x the balance score.
  • The number 7 is called a Lucky Seven. If you win by calling a Lucky Seven bet, the add up is 4x the balance score.
